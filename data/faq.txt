TechFlow Solutions - Frequently Asked Questions

GETTING STARTED

Q: How do I sign up for TechFlow Solutions?
A: Visit www.techflowsolutions.com and click "Start Free Trial". You'll get 14 days free with no credit card required. Simply enter your business email, create a password, and choose your plan.

Q: How long does setup take?
A: Basic setup takes about 15 minutes. Full implementation including data migration typically takes 1-3 days depending on your data volume. Our onboarding team will guide you through every step.

Q: Do you offer a free trial?
A: Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required. You can extend the trial by 7 days if you need more time to evaluate.

Q: Can I import my existing data?
A: Absolutely! We support imports from CSV, Excel, JSON, and direct database connections. We also offer free migration assistance for Enterprise customers.

BILLING AND PAYMENTS

Q: When will I be charged?
A: After your free trial ends, you'll be charged on the 1st of each month for monthly plans, or annually on your signup anniversary for yearly plans.

Q: Can I change my plan anytime?
A: Yes, you can upgrade instantly or downgrade at the end of your billing cycle. Upgrades are prorated, downgrades take effect next billing period.

Q: What if I need to cancel?
A: You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. Your data remains accessible for 90 days after cancellation for export. We also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee.

Q: Do you offer refunds?
A: Yes, we have a 30-day money-back guarantee for new customers. After that, we offer prorated refunds for annual plans if you cancel within 60 days.

TECHNICAL QUESTIONS

Q: Is my data secure?
A: Yes, we use bank-level 256-bit AES encryption, both in transit and at rest. We're SOC 2 Type II certified and undergo annual security audits. All data is backed up in real-time to multiple geographic locations.

Q: What browsers are supported?
A: We support the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Mobile apps are available for iOS (12+) and Android (8+).

Q: What integrations do you offer?
A: We integrate with 100+ popular tools including Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, QuickBooks, Stripe, and many more. Custom integrations are available for Enterprise plans.

Q: Can I use TechFlow on mobile?
A: Yes! We have native iOS and Android apps with full functionality. You can also use our responsive web app on any mobile browser.

Q: What's your uptime guarantee?
A: We guarantee 99.5% uptime for Standard, 99.9% for Professional, and 99.99% for Enterprise plans. Check our status page at status.techflowsolutions.com for real-time updates.
